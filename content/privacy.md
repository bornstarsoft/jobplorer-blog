# Privacy Policy

Jobplorer is currently a static informational website. It provides job site research starting points, country guides, career guides, remote work resources, job seeker tools, and legacy archive content.

Jobplorer currently does not provide user accounts, login features, direct job applications, payment processing, comments, or account dashboards.

## Basic Site Analytics

Jobplorer may use basic site analytics, such as Cloudflare Web Analytics or similar privacy-conscious site analytics, to understand site performance, popular pages, traffic patterns, and technical issues.

Analytics information may include general usage data such as page views, referrers, device or browser information, approximate region, and performance signals. This information is used to improve the website and understand how pages are used.

## Contact Information

If you contact the site owner, information you choose to provide may be used to respond to the inquiry, review corrections, evaluate feedback, or handle related communication.

Please avoid sending sensitive personal information, job applications, payment details, passwords, government identification numbers, or confidential employer information through general contact channels.

## External Links

Jobplorer may link to external job sites, employer pages, public resources, and other third-party websites. External websites have their own privacy policies, terms, cookies, analytics, account systems, payment systems, and application processes.

Review each external website's own policies before using it or submitting information.

## Changes

Jobplorer may update this Privacy Policy as the site changes. Check this page for the current version.
