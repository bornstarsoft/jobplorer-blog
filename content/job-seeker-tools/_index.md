# Job Seeker Tools

Job seeker tools are practical resources for organizing a job search. They may include checklists, comparison worksheets, resume prompts, interview preparation notes, and application tracking ideas.

These tools can help structure your research, but they do not guarantee interviews, offers, salary outcomes, or hiring results. Always adapt templates to your own situation and verify requirements with employers and official sources.
