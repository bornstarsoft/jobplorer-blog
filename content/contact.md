# Contact

Jobplorer welcomes feedback that can help keep the site useful and cautious.

Useful contact reasons may include:

- corrections to outdated or unclear information
- feedback about job site, country guide, career guide, or tool pages
- reports of broken external links
- content suggestions for future Jobplorer guides
- partnership inquiries, without implying any existing partnership

For contact details, please refer to the site owner or repository information.

Please do not send sensitive personal information, job applications, payment details, passwords, government identification numbers, or confidential employer information through general contact channels.

Jobplorer does not process job applications, provide live job listings, guarantee interviews or hiring outcomes, or provide visa, legal, labor, or tax advice.

For more information, review the [Privacy Policy](/privacy/) and [Terms of Use](/terms/).
