# Career Guides

Career guides are practical starting points for exploring job families, skill signals, application preparation, and search strategies.

These guides should help readers ask better questions and verify details with employers, training providers, professional bodies, and official sources. They should not promise hiring outcomes, salary results, or career certainty.

Future guides may cover broad career paths, resume preparation, interviews, portfolio planning, and skill research.
