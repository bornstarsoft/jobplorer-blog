# Software Developer Job Search: Where to Start

Review note: this guide was reviewed using official occupation resources, but requirements, tools, job titles, and market conditions can change. Check official sources and employer postings for current details.

This guide may be useful for job search research around software developer roles. It can be a starting point for comparing broad search channels such as general job sites, professional networking platforms, company career pages, and developer community resources.

Official occupation resources such as the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics Occupational Outlook Handbook and O*NET OnLine can help readers understand broad occupation context. Employer career pages and official job postings should still be checked directly for current role expectations, application details, tools, and job titles.

Portfolio, resume, tool, and role expectations can vary by employer and job posting. Check official sources and employer pages for current details. Requirements, tools, job titles, and market conditions can change.
