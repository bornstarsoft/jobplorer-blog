# Terms of Use

Jobplorer provides general informational content about job sites, country guides, career guides, remote work resources, job seeker tools, and legacy job news archive content.

By using Jobplorer, you understand that the site is a research starting point and not a live job board, recruitment agency, employer, legal adviser, visa adviser, tax adviser, or labor adviser.

## Informational Use Only

Jobplorer content is for general informational purposes only. Details about job sites, employers, job postings, platform policies, pricing, availability, application processes, and local requirements can change.

Always verify information through official websites, current job postings, employer pages, government resources, or qualified professionals when needed.

## No Guarantees or Professional Advice

Jobplorer does not guarantee jobs, interviews, hiring outcomes, salary outcomes, visa eligibility, legal compliance, labor-law outcomes, tax outcomes, or application results.

Jobplorer does not provide visa advice, legal advice, labor advice, tax advice, immigration advice, or professional career counseling.

## External Websites

Jobplorer may link to external job sites, employer pages, public resources, and other third-party websites for convenience.

External job sites and employers control their own postings, policies, pricing, availability, application processes, accounts, data handling, and terms. Jobplorer is not responsible for external website content, changes, policies, or outcomes.

Review each external website's own terms and policies before using it or submitting information.

## Content Updates

Jobplorer content may be updated, corrected, reorganized, archived, or removed over time. Legacy archive pages may remain available to preserve older URLs and historical reference content.

## Responsible Use

Use Jobplorer responsibly as a starting point for your own research. Do not rely on Jobplorer as the only source for employment, legal, visa, labor, tax, or compliance decisions.
