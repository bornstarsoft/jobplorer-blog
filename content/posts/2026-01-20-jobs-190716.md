Gyeongsangnam-do has achieved a remarkable milestone with its employment rate soaring to 63.3%, marking the highest level since the commencement of statistical tracking in 2000. This impressive growth is not just a number; it signifies a robust recovery in the region's job market, with both employment rates rising and unemployment rates falling simultaneously.

In 2025, Gyeongsangnam-do recorded an annual employment figure of 1,799,000, reflecting a significant increase of 4,100 jobs from the previous year. Notably, the male employment rate reached 71.2%, while female employment rose to 55.3%, showcasing a balanced recovery across genders. Furthermore, the youth employment rate has also seen a positive shift, with the unemployment rate for young people dropping to 2.7%.

As we look ahead, the commitment from local authorities to not only maintain this upward trend but also to focus on creating quality jobs and strengthening employment safety nets is crucial. How will these developments shape the future of Gyeongsangnam-do's economy and its workforce?
