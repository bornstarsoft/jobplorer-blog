Special Construction, a leading player in the construction industry, is set to launch its recruitment drive for new and experienced engineers in 2026. With over 55 years of expertise in foundational civil engineering, the company is expanding its reach across the nation, focusing on securing top talent in construction and safety management.

The recruitment will target both fresh graduates and seasoned professionals, with positions available in civil construction and safety management. Notably, the company is offering a substantial incentive for certified professionals, providing an additional monthly allowance of 1 million KRW for those holding technical certifications. This strategic move highlights their commitment to attracting specialized talent in a competitive job market.

As the construction industry evolves, opportunities like this present a significant chance for aspiring engineers to step into impactful roles. Are you ready to take the leap into a rewarding career in construction?
