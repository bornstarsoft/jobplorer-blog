In the rapidly evolving tech landscape, understanding the role of AI in the hiring process has become crucial for aspiring developers. Recently, Incheon Polytechnic University hosted a special lecture titled The Reality of the Development Market and AI, aimed at guiding students in navigating the complexities of the job market shaped by artificial intelligence. The session was led by Lee Seok-kyun, a seasoned professional with over 13 years of experience in various sectors, including e-commerce and startups.

Lee emphasized that today's job market increasingly demands candidates who not only understand AI but can leverage it to solve real-world problems. This insight is particularly valuable for students who may feel overwhelmed by the rapid changes in technology and employment expectations. One student, Kim Bong-jung, expressed newfound clarity about his career path, highlighting the importance of such discussions in alleviating uncertainties.

As educational institutions like Incheon Polytechnic continue to adapt their programs to meet industry needs, the emphasis on practical skills and real-world experience will be vital for students preparing to enter the workforce. How will you leverage AI in your career journey?
