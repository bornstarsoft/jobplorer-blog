In a bold move to enhance its financial competitiveness, Hanwha Investment & Securities has announced the formation of a specialized team, dubbed the 'Flash Team,' aimed at accelerating its transition into a digital asset-focused securities firm. This initiative is part of a broader strategy to expand into new digital business areas and establish global collaboration frameworks, particularly in the realm of Real World Asset (RWA) tokenization.

The Flash Team will comprise skilled professionals capable of executing both domestic and international development projects, transcending traditional boundaries of nationality, affiliation, and rank. This innovative organizational structure is designed to facilitate rapid decision-making and execution, positioning Hanwha as a leader in the burgeoning digital asset space.

With recruitment focused on roles in Web3 RWA development and mobile application development, Hanwha is not just filling positions but is strategically investing in talent that can drive the future of finance. As the company embraces a competency-based evaluation and reward system, it aims to create an environment where developers can thrive and innovate.
