GroupBy, an AI-driven recruitment platform, is set to showcase its groundbreaking solutions at the AI EXPO KOREA 2026, Asia's largest AI event, from May 6 to 8. The company will unveil its next-generation recruitment solutions that leverage artificial intelligence to streamline the hiring process, making it faster and more efficient than ever before.

At the event, attendees can experience live demonstrations of GroupBy's core services, including AI Scout and Recruitment Assistant. The AI Scout feature can identify top candidates based on specific criteria in under a minute, showcasing the platform's impressive speed and accuracy. Additionally, the Recruitment Assistant service manages the entire hiring process, from job description analysis to candidate engagement, significantly reducing the burden on startups.

Founded in 2022, GroupBy has rapidly grown, serving over 2,000 companies and boasting a talent pool of more than 80,000 specialized candidates. With a remarkable 800% annual increase in job seekers on the platform and a tenfold revenue growth in the past two years, GroupBy is redefining recruitment for startups and proving that innovative technology can lead to substantial hiring success.
