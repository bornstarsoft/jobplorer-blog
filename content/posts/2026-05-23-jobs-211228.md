In a groundbreaking move, Google has begun allowing candidates to use AI tools during software engineering interviews. This initiative reflects the growing trend of integrating generative AI into the development process, where a significant portion of new code is now being generated by AI systems. Candidates will be assessed not only on their coding skills but also on their ability to effectively utilize AI for debugging and optimizing existing code.

This pilot program is currently being tested with entry to mid-level positions in select U.S. teams, with plans for broader implementation depending on its success. As the tech landscape evolves, this shift signifies a major change in how companies evaluate talent and adapt to the capabilities of AI.
