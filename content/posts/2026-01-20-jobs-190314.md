Gyeongnam Province has achieved a remarkable milestone in its employment landscape, recording an all-time high employment rate of 63.3% in 2025. This figure not only surpasses the national average of 62.9% but also marks a significant recovery from the lows experienced during the pandemic. The continuous rise in employment rates over the past five years signals a robust rebound in the local job market, showcasing the province's resilience and adaptability.

The increase in employment is complemented by a notable decline in the unemployment rate, which fell to 2.0%, significantly lower than the national average of 2.8%. This dual trend of rising employment and falling unemployment underscores a positive shift in Gyeongnam's economic conditions. Furthermore, the youth employment statistics reveal a promising future, with youth employment rates exceeding 40% and a substantial drop in youth unemployment.

As Gyeongnam celebrates this achievement, officials emphasize the importance of not resting on their laurels. The focus now shifts to creating quality jobs and strengthening employment safety nets to ensure sustainable growth.
