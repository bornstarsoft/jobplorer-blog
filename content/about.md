# About Jobplorer

Jobplorer is an informational resource for exploring job sites, country guides, career guides, remote work resources, and practical job seeker tools around the world.

Jobplorer is not a live job board and does not scrape real-time job listings. It is designed to help readers compare research starting points, understand what to check, and find useful directions for further verification.

The site is being renewed from an older job news summary archive into a broader job information hub. The [Legacy Job News Archive](/posts/) remains available to preserve existing `/posts/` URLs and historical reference content.

Information about job sites, employers, job postings, platform policies, pricing, availability, and local requirements can change. Always check official websites, current job postings, employer pages, and other authoritative sources before applying or making decisions.

Jobplorer does not guarantee employment, interviews, hiring outcomes, salary outcomes, visa eligibility, legal compliance, labor-law outcomes, or tax outcomes. Jobplorer does not provide visa advice, legal advice, labor advice, or tax advice.

For contact options, see [Contact](/contact/). You may also review the [Privacy Policy](/privacy/) and [Terms of Use](/terms/).
